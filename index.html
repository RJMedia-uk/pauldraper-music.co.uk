12/03/2008 - Paul Update & New Work In Progress Track Titles

Hello again, Paul has been in touch lately and has given us some info on what he has been working on. He's also give us some track titles although these are subject to change..

"I took a break from my project after i realized to do what i wanted to do, i.e. do it all myself without a band as such id have to re-equip my studio "Draposound" and I've done some production work to road test it. I did some stuff with Catherine Anne Davies, check her site or myspace and she's got a track called 'Unravel' we worked on."

Paul also went on to reveal some work in progress tracks he has recently been working on, some interesting titles namely Being A Girl Part 3 and Cancer Part II.

"Song titles I've been working on but will probably change are....

Can U Ever Trust Another, Feel Like I Wanna Stay, Friend, Sex With Strangers, Can't Get Fairer Than That, Annie, Being A Girl Part 3, Sociopath, Wont Get Hurt By A Woman Again, Borderline Nymphomaniac, Life Turns On Inconsequential Moments, Synth Pop Hero, Monkeys On A Rock, Say What Everyone Else Thinks And They'll Say Ya Fuckin Mad, cancer pt II."

More info on Paul coming soon, also see the Gallery page on this site for new Draper photos.

10/11/2006 - Read Paul In Uncut Magazine

Decembers issue of Uncut features Paul talking about the break up of his former band Mansun and his new solo project..

"One night it all kicked off. We broke into the wine cellar, picked out the best bottle of champagne and got twatted. There was a lot of shouting and aggro. Chad punched his hand into a brick wall and ended up in hospital on a morphine drip. People were saying that I was a paranoid schizophrenic In fact with all the stress I got a malignant tumour and had to have a course of radiation therapy before having it cut out."
